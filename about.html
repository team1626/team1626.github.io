We are Team 1626

Starting as a team of only 10 members and 2 mentors in September, 2005, 1626 has flourished into a companionship of 45 members, 1 teacher moderator, 2 engineering mentors, and 4 business mentors. We believe that the most important aspect of instilling a love for STEM is early and recurrent exposure to the wonders of both FIRST Robotics and a career in STEM. As a result, we take pride in our mentoring program and recognize it to be a major key of our club activities. We have set up numerous events throughout the year to teach kids both inside and out of school so that they may find their way into the STEM field. 1626 seeks to exemplify this attitude through its inclusion in over 3 pre-season events and business sponsorships as we continue to grow as a team.
